Multimedia, Big Data and Cybersecurity Applications Laboratory

Located in the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Telkom University and operating under the Network, Cybernetics and Engineering Management (NCM) Expertise Group, is a specialized research facility with a multi-faceted focus. Primarily, this field studies the field of multimedia applications, especially Game Technology and Geographic Information Systems (GIS), which utilize cutting-edge technologies to explore interactive simulations and spatial data analysis. Additionally, the lab is actively involved in Big Data efforts, specializing in Machine Learning techniques for broad data analysis and insights. Additionally, its cybersecurity division is divided into three different teams: Red Team, which simulates cyberattacks for security assessments; Blue Team, responsible for improving system defense; and the Cloud Team, dedicated to securing cloud-based data and applications. This multifaceted laboratory is at the forefront of research and development in these important fields.
